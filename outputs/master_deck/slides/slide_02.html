The Pedagogy Gap (Research Framing)

Addressing the "Quality Paradox" in Rural India

Instructional Quality Disparity

Urban Elite: 8 hrs/week high-fidelity instructional time.

Rural/Govt: <2 hrs/week high-fidelity instructional time.

Observation: Enrollment is achieved, but cognitive outcomes remain stagnant due to "Resource Poverty".

The Constraint: Teacher Bandwidth

Traditional prep requires 45 mins/lesson. Rural teachers have <15 mins due to administrative load.

Hypothesis: Voice-first Edge AI can bridge the bandwidth gap via automated contextualization.
