**The Core Problem: Teacher Bandwidth & Efficiency**
Our research shows that the "Quality Gap" in rural education is fundamentally a **"Teacher Bandwidth"** problem.
-   **Data:** 70% of India's 4.9M government teachers work in rural areas, leaving them with **less than 15 minutes of prep time per class** due to administrative overload.
-   **Market Gap:** While the EdTech market is **$10.4B**, it focuses on students. The **₹9,700 Crore** Teacher Enablement market remains critically underserved.

**Our Solution: A Universal Pedagogical Assistant**
SahayakAI is not just a productivity suite; it is a **Voice-First, Multi-Lingual Intelligence Layer** for the Indian classroom.

*   **Voice-First Interface:** Teachers *speak* to the app in their natural language, removing digital literacy barriers.
*   **India-Scale Reach:** Built-in support for **11 Indic Languages**, covering all major state mediums.
*   **Comprehensive K-12:** Agentic support for **All Subjects (Science, Math, Social)** from Class 1 to 12.
*   **Deep Pedagogy:** Our AI agents don't just "generate content"; they are trained on **Bloom's Taxonomy** and **State Curriculum Frameworks** to ensure pedagogical rigor.
*   **Resilient Tech:** Works seamlessly on **Low-Internet (2G)** and affordable hardware.

**Beyond Tools: Building a Community**
Just as the IT community thrives on platforms like StackOverflow, we are building a **"Community of Practice"** for rural teachers.
-   **Knowledge Sharing:** Teachers can share their best lesson plans and strategies.
-   **Peer Support:** An offline-mesh network allows teachers to solve each other's pedagogical challenges, fostering a culture of collective growth.

**Why NSRCEL?**
We need the mentorship of IIM Bangalore to help us structure this not just as a product, but as a scalable social impact institution. We want to learn how to measure and optimize for **Teacher Productivity** and **Learning Outcomes** at scale.

We are **building the Civilizational Intelligence Infrastructure for the next billion learners by transforming rural teachers into 'Super Teachers'.** We would be honored to build it from IIM Bengaluru.
