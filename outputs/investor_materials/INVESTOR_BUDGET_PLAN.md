## 📊 Unit Economics & Scalability

Our "Frugal Innovation" model ensures that as we scale, our efficiency increases.

### Current Unit Economics (Per Teacher)
- **Monthly Cost per DAU**: ₹211 (API + Cloud)
- **Target Government License**: ₹1,000/year (₹83/month)
- **Current Gap**: -₹128/month (Investment Phase)

### The Optimization Roadmap (Target: ₹45/month cost)
1. **Semantic Caching (M3)**: 40% cost reduction by leveraging peer-shared lesson plan hashes.
2. **Pre-Generated Content (M6)**: 30% reduction by using static NCERT visual aid libraries.
3. **Fine-Tuned Small Language Models (M12)**: 50%+ reduction by moving from generic LLMs to specialized pedagogical models.
