## ğŸ› ï¸ Proposed New Global Skills

### 1. `multigrade-planner`
**Goal**: Create a unified 45-min plan for 2-3 grades.
- **Problem**: Teachers can't be in two places at once.
- **Feature**: Logic that schedules "Grade 1: Group Work" while "Grade 2: Teacher Lecture".

### 2. `remediation-engine`
**Goal**: Foundational literacy/numeracy focused tasks.
- **Problem**: Standardized tests are too hard for bridge-learners.
- **Feature**: "Simplify to Foundation" button on any topic.

### 3. `parent-bridge`
**Goal**: Locally-relevant communication.
- **Problem**: Language and literacy barriers with parents.
- **Feature**: Audio-script generator for teachers to send voice notes to parents.

### 4. `admin-express`
**Goal**: Handling non-teaching data work.
- **Problem**: Election/Census work takes over time.
- **Feature**: Automated report drafting and data formatting.
