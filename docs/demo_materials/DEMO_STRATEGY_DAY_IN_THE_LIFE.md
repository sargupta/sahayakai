## *2-Minute Product Demo Strategy*

**Persona:** Mr. Ramesh, a middle-school science teacher in a semi-urban government school in Uttar Pradesh. He has 45 students, limited resources, and is passionate about making science "real" for his kids.

### ðŸ•’ The Scenario: Morning Prep to Afternoon Assessment

#### **Phase 1: The Spark (0:00 - 0:30)**
**Objective:** Show rapid, contextual lesson planning.
*   **Action:** Navigate to **Lesson Plan Generator**.
*   **Interaction:** Use **Voice Input** (Saffron Button).
*   **Prompt:** *"Namaste Sahayak! I need a lesson plan for Class 6 'Changes Around Us'. Focus on reversible and irreversible changes using things students see in a village, like making curd or burning wood."*
*   **Highlight:** Point out the "Local Context" section in the generated plan.

#### **Phase 2: The Vision (0:30 - 1:00)**
**Objective:** Create high-quality visual aids instantly.
*   **Action:** Click the "Generate Visual" shortcut or navigate to **Visual Aid Designer**.
*   **Interaction:** Type a refined request.
*   **Prompt:** *"A clear diagram showing a candle burning versus a candle melting, with labels in Hindi and English. Make it look like a neat blackboard drawing."*
*   **Highlight:** Show the image generation live. Note how it replaces hours of drawing on a board.

#### **Phase 3: The Engagement (1:00 - 1:30)**
**Objective:** Active classroom participation.
*   **Action:** Navigate to **Instant Answer**.
*   **Interaction:** Simulate a student question.
*   **Prompt:** *"Rohan asked: 'Sir, if we freeze the curd, does it become milk again?' Explain why this is irreversible using a simple analogy."*
*   **Highlight:** The explanation should be punchy and relatable (e.g., "Like a broken egg, some things can't be put back together").

#### **Phase 4: The Closing (1:30 - 2:00)**
**Objective:** Homework and Assessment handled.
*   **Action:** Navigate to **Quiz Generator**.
*   **Interaction:** Quick generation.
*   **Prompt:** *"Generate 5 MCQs on 'Changes Around Us' for Class 6. Include one 'Brain Teaser' question about cooking rotis."*
*   **Highlight:** Instant export to PDF/Print. Show the "Bilingual" toggle if available.
