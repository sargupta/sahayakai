# Instructional Design Team

## Mission
To empower teachers by autonomously generating comprehensive, culturally relevant, and pedagogically sound educational materials.

## Team Structure
- **Orchestrator**: `curriculum-director` (Manages the flow, ensures alignment with NCF 2023)
- **Specialists**:
    - `lesson-planner`: Creates detailed 5E lesson plans.
    - `quiz-creator`: Generates Bloom's Taxonomy-aligned assessments.
    - `visual-aid-artist`: Designs blackboard illustrations and diagrams.
    - `rubric-architect`: innovative assessment grids.
    - `worksheet-wizard`: Practice material generator.

## Shared Context
All agents share the following context:
- **Target Audience**: K-12 students in India (CBSE/State Board).
- **Pedagogy**: Constructivist, activity-based learning (5E Model).
- **Constraints**: Low-resource classroom settings (blackboard, simple materials).
